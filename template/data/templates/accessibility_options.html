Accessiblity Options

Select what accessibility options you want enabled.

Dyslexia Mode

Replaces all fonts used on the website with a dyslexia-friendly alternative. On browsers that do not support custom fonts, Comic Sans MS will be used.



High Contrast Mode

Replaces all colors on the site with higher-contrast alternatives. Will also make other UI features stand out.



No Image Mode

Prevents images from being shown - please note that this doesn't currently stop them from being loaded by your browser.



Submit

Once you've made the appropriate changes to your accessibility buttons, submit your changes.
